Parcel not collected

In the unlikely situation that your parcels have not been collected and it is after 18.00 on the scheduled day of collection you can reschedule a collection. Rescheduling automatically re-books collections for the next working day. Collections occur at anytime between 08:30 and 18:30. Unfortunately we cannot provide you with a specific collection time nor advise when the courier will arrive on the day of collection. Please therefore ensure someone is at the premises between these hours to hand the parcel(s) to the driver.

Parcel no delivered?

Delivery Assistance

Typically our customers do not experience any problems with their parcel delivery. However, it is possible on some rare occasions that the package is not delivered due to unforeseen circumstances, including:

  • Traffic Issues: these include, congestion, road closure, traffic accidents which necessitate courier taking a longer route.
  • Adverse weather conditions which can force the courier to change their usual route
