Same Day Delivery Vehicle Guidelines

Depending on the size of your parcel, your document / parcel may be collected by either motorbike, small, medium or large van. Our system will determine the most suitable form of transport for your item depending on size and weight, so you can be sure you are always offered the most cost effective option. For your reference below are the size guidelines which determine what sort of vehicle your item will be collected by.

Motorbike - 30cm x 30cm x 30cm
Small van - 150cm x 135cm x 105cm
Medium van - 220cm x 150cm x 125cm
Large van - 310cm x 150cm x 150cm
